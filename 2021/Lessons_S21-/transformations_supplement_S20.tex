$f(x)=\sqrt{x}$\\
~\\
Domain: $x\geq 0$ or $[0,\infty)$\\
~\\
Range: $y\geq 0$ or $[0,\infty)$\\
~\\
$x-$intercept at $(0,0)$\\
~\\
$y-$intercept at $(0,0)$\\
